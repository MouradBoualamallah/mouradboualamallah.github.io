My story

I began my career in media in 1996 as a journalist for Algerian newspapers such as 'Al-Shorouk' and 'Al-Asil', then as a presenter and producer in private audiovisual production firms for the benefit of Algerian public television and other organizations such as ministries and national associations. During that time, I hosted various television shows and completed a big number of documentaries.


2002, I relocated to Dubai to work as a journalist in the economics department for the MBC satellite channel, and later as an anchor in the economics department for Al Arabiya. During that time, I covered a number of big economic events in the UAE, including meetings of the International Monetary Fund and the World Bank.
